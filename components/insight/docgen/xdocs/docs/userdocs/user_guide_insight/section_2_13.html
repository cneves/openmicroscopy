The Measurement Tool significantly extends the functionality of the Image Viewer, by allowing you to perform a variety of measurement tasks and analyses on your image.

These include:

  • Defining Regions of Interest (ROIs) on the image - and on each z-section or timepoint of multi-dimensional images;
  • Annotating ROIs with textual annotations;
  • Assigning stroke and fill colours to shapes;
  • Getting measurements of the ROI shapes, including area, length and angle;
  • Plotting intensity values;
  • Loading existing ROIs and saving ROIs to file; and
  • Managing ROIs - editing shapes, viewing, deleting and adding.
