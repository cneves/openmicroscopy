To delete an individual ROI shape:

  1. Select the shape to be deleted by clicking on the shape in the Image Viewer window (for rectangles, ellipses and polygons, clicking inside the shape will select it).
  2. Then
    • Either press the Delete key on your keyboard to delete the selected shape;
    • Or open the ROI Manager, right click on the selected ROI shape, select ROI Management Options and then Delete.
