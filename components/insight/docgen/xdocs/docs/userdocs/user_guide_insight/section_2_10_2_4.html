In the Hierarchy Viewer, you can perform management actions on a single image or multiple images:

  • Categorise or decategorise an image or selection of images.
  • View and edit annotations for an image or selection of images.
  • View property information of an individual image.
  • Remove an image from its current dataset.
To perform any of these tasks, you can:
  • Select one or more images in a container, and then open the Edit menu to select the operation of your choice.
  • Or, right-click on a single image to see the Edit menu as above.
Icons are used to indicate the presence of category and annotations for any image appearing as a thumbnail in the Hierarchy Viewer; this iconic information is preserved for pinned up thumbnails.

Clicking on an icon will bring up the corresponding tab in the Working area (see Section 3.10.2.5)
