This Beta version of OMERO.importer and OMERO.insight has been rigorously tested to ensure it works as expected. Errors however may occur and a window will appear (Figure 4.2-1) inviting you to submit feedback about the error.

Providing this information is not required, but will help the development team identify and overcome the cause of the error. Submitting your email address is optional, and if you do, this information will only be used to contact you for more details about what you were doing when the error occurred.
