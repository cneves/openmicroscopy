Various changes to the visual display of a selected ROI shape can be made through the ROI Shape Inspector (see Figure 3.10.5.4-1).
 
Figure 3.10.5.4-1: ROI shape Inspector view

To bring up the Inspector view adjust the appearance of an ROI shape:

  1. Select the ROI shape, either by clicking on it in the Image Viewer window, or by selecting it in the ROI Manager or ROI Results view.
  2. Select the Inspector tab.
  3. Current display settings for the ROI shape will be displayed.
