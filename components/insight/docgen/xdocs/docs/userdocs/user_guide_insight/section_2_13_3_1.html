In OMERO, we use the following definitions:

  • ROI - a multi-dimensional object, consisting of one or more ROI shapes, indicated by the icon: .
  • ROI shape - a two dimensional object that appears on one plane only, indicated by the icon: .
An ROI shape also automatically exists as an ROI. An ROI across multiple dimensions (z-sections or timepoints) will be created when an individual ROI shape is selected and propagated.

Propagating ROI shapes to create multi-dimensional ROIs is discussed more in Section 3.10.3.4. Viewing and managing ROIs and ROI shapes is discussed more in Section 3.10.5 and in Section 3.10.3.6.
